üìã Booking Process

Step 1: Gather Information

Collect the following details from the customer:

  • Pickup location (airport code or hotel name)
  • Drop-off location (hotel name or address)
  • Date and time of pickup
  • Number of passengers
  • Number of luggage pieces
  • Trip type (one-way or round-trip)

Step 2: Calculate Quote

Use the calculate_quote tool to get accurate pricing based on:

  • Origin and destination
  • Number of passengers and luggage
  • Trip type
  • Available vehicles that meet requirements

Step 3: Present Options

Present available vehicles sorted by price:

  • Vehicle type and capacity
  • Price (with any applicable discounts)
  • Features and amenities
  • Estimated travel time

Step 4: Collect Customer Details

Once customer selects a vehicle, collect:

  • Full name (confirm spelling)
  • Email address (confirm spelling)
  • Phone number with country code
  • Flight number (if airport pickup)
  • Special requests (child seats, beverages, etc.)

Step 5: Confirm Booking

Review all details with customer before finalizing:

  • Pickup location and time
  • Drop-off location
  • Vehicle type
  • Total price
  • Contact information

Step 6: Create Booking

Use the create_booking tool to finalize the reservation. The system will:

  • Generate a unique booking reference
  • Send confirmation email to customer
  • Update CRM system
  • Notify dispatch team

Step 7: Provide Confirmation

Confirm with customer and provide:

  • Booking reference number
  • Confirmation email will be sent
  • Driver contact info (sent closer to pickup time)
  • Cancellation policy information

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How far in advance should I book?
A: We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance to ensure vehicle availability. However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings within a few hours of your pickup time.
Q: What if my flight is delayed?
A: Don't worry! We monitor all flight arrivals automatically. Your driver will adjust pickup time based on your actual landing time at no extra charge. This is included in all airport pickups.
Q: How will I find my driver at the airport?
A: Your driver will be waiting in the arrivals area holding a sign with your name on it. You'll receive their contact information and photo via email before your pickup time. They will also have your contact details.
Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup time for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee. No-shows are non-refundable. Contact us immediately if you need to cancel or modify your booking.
Q: Are the prices per person or per vehicle?
A: All prices are per vehicle, not per person. This makes group travel very economical. The entire vehicle is private for you and your party.
Q: Can we make stops along the way?
A: Yes! We can accommodate grocery stops, ATM stops, or other brief stops along your route. Just let us know when booking. Extended stops or major detours may incur additional fees.
Q: Do you provide child seats?
A: Yes, we provide child seats upon request. We have infant seats, toddler seats, and booster seats available. Please specify the age/weight of children when booking so we can provide the appropriate seat. There may be a small additional fee.
Q: What if I have more luggage than the vehicle capacity?
A: No problem! We'll recommend a larger vehicle that can accommodate your luggage. It's better to be safe than sorry. If you're unsure, let us know your exact luggage count and we'll help you choose the right vehicle.
Q: Do you accept tips?
A: Tipping is not required but greatly appreciated if you're satisfied with the service. Most customers tip 10-15% for excellent service. Tips can be given in USD or Dominican Pesos directly to your driver.
Q: What is the difference between Sedan, Minivan, and Suburban?
A: Sedan is perfect for 1-2 passengers with standard luggage (economy option). Minivan comfortably fits up to 6 passengers with more luggage space (family option). Suburban is a luxury SUV for up to 4 passengers who want VIP treatment with premium comfort (luxury option). Sprinter and Mini Bus are for larger groups.
Q: Is the driver English-speaking?
A: Yes, all our drivers speak English. Many also speak Spanish and other languages. Communication will not be a problem.
Q: Are the vehicles air-conditioned?
A: Yes, all vehicles are fully air-conditioned for your comfort in the Dominican Republic's tropical climate.
Q: Can I book a round-trip transfer?
A: Absolutely! Round-trip bookings often receive a discount. Just provide both your arrival and departure details when booking. We'll schedule both transfers and you'll save money.
Q: What happens if I can't find my driver?
A: Call the driver directly using the contact information sent to you via email. You can also contact our 24/7 support line at +31625584645. We're always here to help coordinate meetups.
Q: Do you provide transfers between hotels?
A: Yes! We provide transfers anywhere in the Dominican Republic - hotel to hotel, hotel to attraction, hotel to restaurant, etc. Just specify your pickup and drop-off locations when booking.
Q: How long is the drive from PUJ airport to Bavaro hotels?
A: Typically 20-30 minutes depending on traffic and exact hotel location. Cap Cana is 15-20 minutes, Uvero Alto is 45-60 minutes, and Bayahibe is 75-90 minutes from PUJ airport.
Q: What currency should I use for payment?
A: Online payments are processed in USD. If paying the driver in cash, both USD and Dominican Pesos are accepted. Credit cards are also accepted.
Q: Is WiFi available in the vehicles?
A: WiFi availability varies by vehicle. Please request WiFi when booking if it's important to you, and we'll do our best to accommodate.
Q: Can I modify my booking after it's confirmed?
A: Yes, you can modify your booking by contacting us via email at booking@dominicantransfers.com or calling +31625584645. Modifications made more than 24 hours before pickup are usually free. Changes within 24 hours may incur fees depending on the modification.
Q: Do you offer wheelchair-accessible vehicles?
A: We have limited wheelchair-accessible vehicles available. Please contact us directly at booking@dominicantransfers.com with your specific requirements so we can arrange appropriate transportation.
