How This Works with Google Ads

  • Use {keyword} placeholder in your Google Ads Final URL
  • Google automatically replaces it with the user's search term
  • Your landing page dynamically personalizes based on that keyword
  • This creates perfect ad-to-page relevance for maximum Quality Score
  • All chat functionality remains unchanged - AI handles conversations normally
